About

Community of authors archive is a website built on a community founded completely online. A non-profit and non-commercial archive of original work. The archive aims to provide a platform for authors to share their works, connect with readers, and foster a community of literary enthusiasts.

The website is completely built off of an online community which created the goal to recognize small authors and writer who wish to publish or preview their writing freely and promote their own profiles and literary work.
